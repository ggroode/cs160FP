Mashed Potatoes

Average Rating: 3.7/5
How would you rate this recipe?
Ingredients | Yields: cups
  1. 2 pounds baking potatoes
  2. 2 tablespoon butter
  3. 1 cup milk
  4. To taste: salt and pepper
Steps | 25 min
  1. Rinse the potatoes.
  2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes; drain.
  3. In a small saucepan heat butter and milk over low heat until butter is melted. Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into potatoes until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
