Help Page

  • All users will first be asked to {% if user.is_authenticated %} login {% else %} login {% endif %} to their account.
  • To browse through an assortment of recipes, click on the “Browse Recipes” button in the Navigation Bar at the very top. This will take you to a page displaying several recipes, where you can customize search results by using filters. You can view the filters when you click on the filter icon. When you find your desired meals/recipes, you can click on the + icon to add them to your meal planner. Clicking on a specific recipe from your meal planner will take you to the corresponding recipe page containing the relevant details for that recipe.
  • To create or upload your own recipe, click the “Create Recipe” button on the navigation bar. This will take you to a page where you can add an image of your recipe and provide details like the name, description, ingredients, instructions, as well as any relevant tags. Once you are done, you can publish your recipe so that it is accessible to the community.
